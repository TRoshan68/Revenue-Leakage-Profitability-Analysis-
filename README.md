# 📊 Revenue Leakage & Profitability Analysis
### SQL Server · Power BI · DAX · Star Schema · Data Modelling

> Detecting hidden revenue losses across 25,000+ transactions (2020–2025)  
> to protect margins, optimise discounts, and support strategic decisions.

---

## 🔍 Problem Statement
Many organisations experience revenue growth on paper while silently losing 
margins through discounts, returns, and operational inefficiencies. This 
project uncovers **where, why, and how much revenue is leaking** — and what 
to do about it.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Detect and quantify revenue leakage across products, regions & segments
- Evaluate the impact of discount strategies on profitability
- Identify return-driven losses by customer segment and product category
- Deliver actionable, governance-ready insights via interactive dashboards

---

## 📁 Dataset
| Dimension | Details |
|---|---|
| Transactions | 25,000+ sales records |
| Time Period | 2020 – 2025 (6 years) |
| Dimensions | Date · Product · Customer · Region |
| Key Metrics | Revenue · Profit · Discounts · Returns · Leakage |

---

## 🗃️ Data Model
- Designed a **Star Schema** with optimised fact and dimension tables
- Implemented primary & foreign keys for referential integrity
- Enabled multidimensional analysis across time, region, product & customer

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- **2022** had the highest revenue — and the highest leakage, revealing the 
  hidden cost of rapid growth
- Discounts **above 20%** showed a measurable decline in profit margins
- Certain regions and product categories contributed disproportionately to 
  return-related losses
- **Consumer segment** exhibited higher return rates vs. corporate clients

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Executive overview: Revenue · Profit · Margin · Leakage KPIs
- Leakage breakdown by product, region & discount level
- Time intelligence: YoY · YTD · Rolling trends
- Row-Level Security (RLS) for governance-compliant, audit-ready reporting
- Interactive slicers and bookmarks for dynamic exploration
